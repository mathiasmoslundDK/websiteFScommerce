About FS Commerce — e-commerce consulting led by Mathias Moslund.

FS Commerce LLC is a Delaware-registered single-member LLC providing e-commerce growth consulting to small and mid-sized direct-to-consumer brands. The company was formed in March 2025 and operates out of Granada Hills, California.

The practice is owned and operated by Mathias Dahl Moslund, who works directly with each client. Engagements are structured as multi-month strategic retainers, typically ranging from $2,000 to $10,000 per month depending on scope.

Clients are primarily US-based DTC brands operating on Shopify and similar platforms. The company's California presence enables direct, in-person engagement with clients on the US West Coast.

04FAQ
The conversations we always have.

Questions worth answering before a first call.

Who does FS Commerce work with?+
Small and mid-sized e-commerce businesses, primarily in the United States, with a focus on direct-to-consumer brands operating on Shopify and similar platforms.
What services do you offer?+
E-commerce growth strategy and roadmap development, performance marketing strategy (paid acquisition, retention, scaling), operational consulting for scaling DTC brands, and data analysis and conversion optimization.
How are engagements structured?+
Multi-month strategic retainers tailored to each client's growth stage and objectives. Typical client engagements range from $2,000 to $10,000 per month.
How do clients pay?+
Retainer payments are received in USD by ACH, wire transfer, or card. All engagements are documented in a written agreement before any work begins.
Where is FS Commerce based?+
FS Commerce LLC is a Delaware-registered single-member LLC, with US operations based in Granada Hills, California. The company holds a valid EIN and is in good standing with the State of Delaware.
How do I get started?+
Send an email to mathiasmoslund@gmail.com with a short description of your brand and what you're hoping to work on. We'll schedule a short introduction call.
